Sandra Schlicker, DiplPsych, PP

Research Associate

Friedrich-Alexander-University Erlangen-Nuremberg

Information

Sandra Schlicker is a licensed psychotherapist and research associate at the University of Erlangen-Nürnberg. Her research primarily focuses on Internet-based interventions for depression in patients with chronic medical conditions.
